FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?
+

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?
+

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers free challenges and resources. There are also premium features available for advanced learning and additional benefits.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?
+

Absolutely! All Frontend Mentor projects are designed to be portfolio-worthy. You can showcase your solutions and demonstrate your coding skills to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?
+

Frontend Mentor has an active community where you can ask questions, get feedback, and collaborate with other developers. You can also access solutions and resources for guidance.
